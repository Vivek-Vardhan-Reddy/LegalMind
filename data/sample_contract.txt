AND 
VVR Consulting Services, located at Bangalore, India (“Consultant”).

The Consultant agrees to provide software development and technical consulting services as

The Consultant agrees to maintain confidentiality of all proprietary information obtained 
during the term of this Agreement. This obligation shall survive indefinitely after termination

The Consultant waives all moral rights and claims to such intellectual property. 
5. Term and Termination 
This Agreement shall remain in effect for a period of 3 years. 
The Company may terminate this Agreement at any time without cause and without prior 
notice. 
The Consultant may terminate this Agreement only with 90 days written notice and 
Company approval.

The Consultant shall be liable for any direct, indirect, incidental, special, or consequential 
damages arising from services rendered under this Agreement. 
There shall be no monetary cap on the Consultant’s liability. 
7. Non-Compete 
The Consultant agrees not to provide similar services to any competitor of the Company for 
a period of 5 years after termination of this Agreement. 
8. Dispute Resolution 
Any disputes shall be resolved exclusively in the courts located in Hyderabad, India. 
The Company reserves the right to seek injunctive relief in any jurisdiction it deems 
appropriate.
